Publications

[1] Khan, M. H., Hosseini, S., Minaei-Bidgoli, B., & Li, X. (2025). ASCENT: Interpretable Aspect-Based Sentiment Analysis via Contrastive Learning and Neuron-Level Optimization. Submitted to IEEE Transactions on Knowledge and Data Engineering (TKDE).

[2] Cherkt, R., Khan, M. H., Sheikh, A. A., & Li, H. (2025). Academic Integrity and Ethical Awareness in LLM Use: Multi-Group Analysis Across Cultures and Education Levels. Submitted to Journal of Educational Computing Research (JOECR).

Investigates how perceived ethical risks, transparency, cultural orientation, and awareness of academic integrity shape the ethical awareness of LLM use among students across various educational levels and cultures. Results highlight the strong role of cultural orientation and integrity in fostering responsible AI engagement.
